# Teleflow
A voice-driven teleprompter designed for natural speaking.

Teleflow is a voice-driven teleprompter designed for natural speaking.

With built-in speech recognition, Teleflow can automatically scroll along with your voice as you speak. You can also manually adjust the teleprompter progress at any time, making it easy to stay on track during recording, presenting, or rehearsal.

To make the reading experience more comfortable, Teleflow includes adjustable transparency and font size, so you can tune the display to fit your setup and speaking style.
